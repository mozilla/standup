Clients

You can use Standup with any officially certified Standup client!

  • standup-irc
  • A client you write!

In-context help

!help to your irc bot will get you a list of commands, including how to make and join teams.

Status message syntax

Standup supports the following syntax in status messages:

#hashtags
Just like other social media services, Standup supports hashtags! Some hashtags have special meaning. All hashtags get re-printed at the beginning of your status message.
bug #n and bug n
Will linkify this to the Mozilla Bugzilla url.
pull #n and pull n
Linkifies pull requests to their project-specific pull url.
